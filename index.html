Good to know

The details, before you book

Do I need to book in advance or can I show up?
Yes — and that's the whole point. We reserve your table at all 3 bars in advance so you walk in without waiting or planning. Walk-ins aren't possible because every guest's route is coordinated with the venues.
Is there a guide or is it really self-guided?
Truly self-guided. There's no guide and no group. You receive your route in your inbox right after booking, then walk it at your own pace. Most guests finish in 3–4 hours.
What's included in the price?
One drink at each of three hand-picked bars — chosen from the special WOGO menu, with 3 cocktails (or mocktails) to pick from at every stop — plus reservations made for you at all three venues and a digital route map with addresses and timings. No app needed.
I don't drink alcohol — can I still join?
Absolutely. Mocktails (alcohol-free cocktails) are available at every bar on every route, so non-drinkers can enjoy the full experience too.
Is it suitable for groups, bachelorettes, or birthdays?
Yes — most of our guests come in groups of 2–8. Bigger groups (8+) welcome too; just contact us before booking so we can coordinate timings with the bars. Gift cards are a great fit for celebrations.
Can I reschedule if my plans change?
Yes — you can reschedule your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your start time. Just contact us and we'll move you to another available date. Within 24 hours of your start time, bookings are final because your tables are already reserved at each bar.
Do I need to be 18?
Yes — Dutch law requires all guests to be 18+ to drink alcohol. Bring ID, just in case. Under-18s are welcome on the mocktail version when accompanied by an adult — contact us to arrange.
